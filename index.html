Snehal Jauhri

I'm a Postdoctoral researcher at the 3D AI Lab at TU Munich, where my research focuses on 3D understanding and generation, reinforcement learning, and embodied AI. I completed my PhD in Robot Perception and Learning at TU Darmstadt with the PEARL Lab, advised by Dr. Georgia Chalvatzaki. I hold a Master's degree from TU Delft, and previously interned at the Allen Institute for AI (PRIOR team). My work has been recognized with the Best Mobile Manipulation Paper Award at IROS 2022 and a Best Paper Finalist at the H2R Workshop, CoRL 2025.

Research

My research lies at the intersection of computer vision, robot learning, and 3D scene understanding. I'm interested in how embodied agents can perceive, reason about, and interact with the 3D world — using neural fields, reinforcement learning, and large pretrained models.
